Customer Turnover (or Churn) is an popular topic for subscription-based industries (e.g. T-Mobile, Netflix), particularly in telecommunications.

In this project, I used a dataset from a fictional telco company (from Kaggle & IBM) to train classification models.

The models can be deployed to determine whether a customer is likely to churn based on the probability assigned by the model to each customer in the dataset.
